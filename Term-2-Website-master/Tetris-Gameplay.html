This is a small version of Tetris that you can try

Tetris is an easy game and has simple rules. Your goal is to play as long as possible before game over and score as many points as possible.

  1. You can only move the peices horizontally and rotate it
  2. Your game is over when you reach to the top
  3. You can only remove the peices by filling a line
